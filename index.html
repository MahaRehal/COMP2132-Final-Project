To hide/show the instructions and rules click here

Instructions:

  • The game goes on for 3 rounds, after which your total score will be counted
  • To start game click the New Game button
  • Click Roll Dice to start a new round
  • Click End Game button to view the total score
  • After the score summary, you can restart game by clicking the New Game button

    • Rules:

  • If any of the two dice comes up as a 1 then the score for that round is 0.
  • If both dice are the same number, the score is the total of the two dice times 2.
  • Otherwise the round score is the sum of both dice
